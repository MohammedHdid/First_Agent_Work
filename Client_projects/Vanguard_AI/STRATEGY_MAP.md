# STRATEGY MAP: High-End AI SaaS (B2B) Waitlist

This map links the core project parameters and Customer Uncertainties to specific UI Components and psychological triggers for our "Unicorn" Landing Page.

## Project Parameters
- **Niche**: High-End AI SaaS (B2B)
- **Visual Identity**: Autonomous "Unicorn" Design (Glassmorphism, Light-leaks, Framer Motion)
- **Offer Structure**: Digital Leads / Waitlist Capture
- **Nervous System**: Manual/Mock API (No live N8N/Supabase initially)

## Customer Uncertainty Mapping

### 1. Uncertainty: "Is this just another generic AI wrapper?"
**Solution: The 'Unicorn' Hero Section (Authority & Polish)**
- **UI Component**: Full-screen hero with an immersive dark-mode glowing gradient background (Light-leaks).
- **Copy Engine Hook**: Problem-Agitate-Solve (PAS) focusing on B2B inefficiency vs. our bespoke AI engine.
- **Micro-interaction**: Magnetic, glowing "Join Waitlist" button using Framer Motion.

### 2. Uncertainty: "Will this actually integrate into my enterprise workflow?"
**Solution: The Abstract Visualization Section (Clarity)**
- **UI Component**: A sleek, animated bento-grid or technical mock-up showing API endpoints or an abstract data flow.
- **Copy Engine Hook**: Bulleted, high-contrast metrics ("Save 10+ hours/week").

### 3. Uncertainty: "Who else is trusting this? What is the risk?"
**Solution: The "Exclusive Club" Social Proof (Trust)**
- **UI Component**: A subtle, scrolling marquee of "Supported by" or trusted partner logos.
- **Secondary Component**: A clear statement: "Join leading pioneers on the waitlist."

### 4. Uncertainty: "What happens when I give you my email?"
**Solution: Transparent Frictionless Lead Capture (Action)**
- **UI Component**: Inline waitlist form powered by a localized mock API.
- **Feedback Loop**: Immediate success toast/modal with a premium Framer Motion pop-in effect: "Welcome to the Vanguard. We'll be in touch."
